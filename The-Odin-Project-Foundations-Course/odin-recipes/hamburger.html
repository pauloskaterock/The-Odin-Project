Hamburger Recipes Tutorial

Hamburger By pauloskaterock

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices of cheese
  • Lettuce, tomato, and onion for toppings

Instructions

  1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a bowl, mix ground beef, salt, and pepper. Form into 4 patties.
  3. Grill patties for 4-5 minutes on each side, adding cheese slices in the last minute.
  4. Toast hamburger buns on the grill.
  5. Assemble burgers with your favorite toppings.
