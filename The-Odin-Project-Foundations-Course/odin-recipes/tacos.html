Tacos By pauloskaterock

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 8 small tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/2 cup sour cream

Instructions

  1. In a skillet, cook ground beef over medium heat until browned.
  2. Add taco seasoning and water. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Warm tortillas in a separate pan or microwave.
  4. Assemble tacos by adding beef, lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, and sour cream.
